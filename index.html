About ICERECT-22

The proposed international conference is very much relevant now because, the present technological developments in the areas of computers, mobile, Digital Media, E-Commerce, Data Science, Artificial Intelligence, Robotics, Industrial Automation, IoT, Industry 4.0, Cryptography, and Blockchain have enhanced research activities globally.  At present relatively more research Scholars are pursuing Ph.D. research work in the reputed universities in this area compared to any other area of Engineering. There is a very high demand for jobs in the areas of Artificial Intelligence, Data Science, Machine learning, and Network security. The faculty members of Electronics, IT-related and Electrical branches are very keen to enhance their knowledge in the above emerging areas to cope up with the new technological developments. This International conference ICERECT will focus more research papers in the above areas from the researchers of Indian and foreign universities and from the industries. This conference will be highly useful for the faculty, students and researchers.

All the selected papers were presented and the deliberations were more useful to the research scholars. A number of researchers from both academic institutions and industries had contributed their novel methods, algorithms, analysis techniques to solve various problems in the above field. ICERECT-15

The second International conference on Emerging Research in electronics, Computer science and Technology (ICERECT-15) was organized during 17th to 19th December 2015 and it was technically sponsored by IEEE Computer Society, Bangalore Section. The conference provided an opportunity for the Research scholars and faculty of various institutions to demonstrate their research talents in the emerging area of Electrical, Electronics, Computer and Information Technology related field. The response to the conference has been overwhelming with more than 86 technical papers presented in 16 sessions of three days. The response has been amazing. The content of the proceeding covers broad area like VLSI, Image processing, Pattern recognition, Big Data Analytics, Multimedia, Computer networks, Bio-medical Engineering, Medical electronics, Computer networks, Power electronics and Industrial drives. All papers have been strictly reviewed and at least two eminent referees were approached for every contributed paper. Review reports were critical and positive. All the selected and presented papers in the conference were published in IEEE Explore with the ISBN 978-1-4673-9563-2.

ICERECT-18

Third International Conference on Emerging Research in Electronics, Computer Science and Technology was organized during 22nd to 24th August 2018 and has witnessed research papers from diverse area of Electronics, Computer Science and Information Technology covering important aspects in these areas. On behalf of the conference committee, we sincerely thank all the delegates, reviewers, session chairs, management, participants, volunteers, TEQIP –III and Springer for their wonderful support.

Submissions

Instructions for authors:

  1. Papers must be formatted using the IEEE conference paper template. Paper must be submitted in English only.

  2. Papers submitted for review must clearly state: The purpose of the work, To what extent it advances the state-of-the-art, Specific results and their impact, Use only Title Case for the paper title, i.e. capitalising all main words.

  3. Do not use academic titles such as Dr., Professor, HoD, Director, etc. in your final paper.

  4. Kindly note that Fourth International Conference on Emerging Research in Electronics, Computer Science and Technology (ICERECT-2022) will follow a "double-blind" review process. All submitted papers will be judged based on their quality, presentation and relevance through a "double-blind" review process, where the identities of the authors are withheld from the reviewers. Therefore, the authors are required to preserve the anonymity of submission, while at the same time allowing the reader to fully gain the context of related past work, including their own. Papers that do not conform with "double-blind submission" policies will be rejected without review (Very Important!). Please read the guidelines (given below) to preserve anonymity with double-blind submission.

  5. Since the "Accepted and presented papers" of Fourth International Conference on Emerging Research in Electronics, Computer Science and Technology (ICERECT-2022) will be submitted for possible publication in the IEEE Xplore® Digital Library (Conference Record # 56837), the manuscript must strictly adhere to the standard IEEE two column format and must not be more than 8 pages.

  6. Typesetting instructions and sample templates: IEEE Manuscript Templates for Conference Proceedings.

  7. The Camera Ready Copy (CRC) of every "accepted" paper will be subjected to the "similarity check" using IEEE CrossCheck and Prohibited Authors List (PAL). The CRC that fails these checks would be rejected.

Guidelines to preserve anonymity for "double-blind" submissions:

  1. Remove the names and affiliations of authors from the title page of the PDF file that you submit for review. This information must be included in the final camera ready copy if your paper gets accepted. While submitting your paper for review, you need to include all the author details only at the registration page. The PDF file of the paper should not have the author information.


  2. Remove acknowledgments of identifying names and funding sources from the PDF file that you will submit for review. This information must be included in the final camera ready copy if your paper gets accepted.


  3. Remove project titles or names that can be used to trace back to the authors via a web search engine.


  4. Use care in referring to your previous works as related works. Do not omit references to provide anonymity, as this leaves the reviewer unable to grasp the context. Instead, you can reference your past work in the third person format, just like any other piece of related work by another author. For example, instead of "Recently, we have proposed a new protocol [3] ...," sentences in the spirit of “Recently, the authors in [3] have proposed a new protocol ..." may be used.


  5. Papers with the same title and abstract should not be posted on a public website, such as arxiv.org, or shared via public mailing lists or communicated to other conferences/journals/publications.


Important IEEE policy:

  1. IEEE reserves the right to exclude a paper from distribution after the conference (including its removal from IEEE Xplore) if the paper is not presented at the conference.


  2. Papers are reviewed on the basis that they do not contain plagiarized material and have not been submitted to any other conference at the same time (double submission). These matters are taken very seriously and the IEEE will take action against any author who engages in either practice.
