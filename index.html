About

P.E.S. College of Engineering (PESCE), Mandya was established in 1962, with the initiation of Late Sri. K.V.Shankara Gowda, the founder President and Ex-Education Minister of Government of Karnataka. The Institution is managed by Peoples Education Trust®, under the leadership of Dr. H.D. Chowdaiah, Ex. MLA. It is one of the Pioneer Technical Institutes in the country and has gained a very good reputation by some outstanding achievements of the faculty and students. The institution is functioning under the Grant-in-Aid scheme of Government of Karnataka, permanently affiliated to Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU), Belgaum, become autonomous in the year 2008. It is recognized by All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi and is accredited by National Board of Accreditation (NBA), New Delhi. PESCE has taken 30th place in Outlook Express All India Ranking. State ranking and National Ranking of PESCE in Career 360 is ‘AA+’ and ‘A’ respectively. The institute has nine Under Graduate programs and eight Post Graduate programs. The institution has the best campus with state-of-the-art and National standard sports facilities.

P.E.S. College of Engineering (PESCE), Mandya, has conducted several international conferences on emerging trends to trigger the minds of researchers, faculty members and also students.

With the rich experience in organizing National and International conferences, the Department of Electronics & Communication, Computer science and Engineering, Electrical & Electronics and Information Science & Engineering, is now conducting Third International conference on Emerging Research in Electronics, Computer science and Technology - ICERECT-18, during 23rd and 24th August 2018.

We would like to invite you to contribute to and participate in Third International conference on Emerging Research in Electronics, Computer science and Technology - ICERECT-18, which will be held during 23rd and 24th August 2018.

2018 International conference on Emerging Research in electronics, Computer science and Technology - ICERECT-18 is a forum for scientists, engineers and researchers to discuss and exchange novel ideas, results, experiences and work-in-process on all aspects of Electronics, Computer Science and Information Technology. The aim of the conference is to provide a platform to the researchers and practitioners from both academia as well as industry to meet and share cutting-edge development in the related field. The Conference primarily looks for significant contributions in the broad areas of Electronics, Computer Science and Information Technology in theoretical and practical aspects. It brings together experts from industry, governments and academia, experienced in engineering, design and research.

In the previous conferences ICERECT has attracted many high quality research papers spanning over the various aspects of Electronics, Computer Science and Information Technology. Having the rich experience in organizing International conferences over the years, ICERECT has developed into a well established conference bringing together academic scientists, professors, research scholars and students to share and disseminate information on knowledge and scientific research works related to Electronics, Computer Science and Information Technology.

The conference will provide the authors and listeners with great opportunities for national and international collaboration and networking among universities and institutions from India and abroad for promoting research and developing technologies. It also aims to bring together researchers and scientists to discuss problems and solutions in the area, to identify new issues, and to shape future directions for research.

The ICERECT-18 offers a rich program including keynote speeches, plenary sessions, and regular presentations. We need your contribution and participation to make it a successful event.

    P.E.S. College of Engineering (PESCE), Mandya was established in 1962, with the initiation of Late Sri. K.V.Shankara Gowda, the founder President and Ex-Education Minister of Government of Karnataka. The Institution is managed by Peoples Education Trust®, under the leadership of Dr. H.D. Chowdaiah, Ex. MLA. It is one of the Pioneer Technical Institutes in the country and has gained a very good reputation by some outstanding achievements of the faculty and students. It is recognized by All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi and is accredited by National Board of Accreditation (NBA), New Delhi. PESCE has taken 30th place in Outlook Express All India Ranking. State ranking and National Ranking of PESCE in Career 360 is ‘AA+’ and ‘A’ respectively. The Department of Electronics & Communication, Computer science and Engineering, Electrical & Electronics and Information Science & Engineering, with their rich experience has conducted two international conferences on emerging trends to trigger the minds of researchers, faculty members and also students.

    ICERECT-12

    The First International conference on Emerging Research in electronics, Computer science and Technlogy (ICERECT-12) was held during Dec-2012 and the proceedings were published in Springer. The aim of the conference was to provide a platform to the researchers and practitioners from both academia as well as industry to meet and share cutting-edge development in the related field. The Conference looked for significant contributions to all major fields of the Electronics, Computer Science and Information Technology in theoretical and practical aspects. The Conference focused to provide an excellent international forum for sharing knowledge and results on the most recent and relevant research, theory, methodology and applications of Electronics, Computer Science Engineering and Applications. We had invited a distinguished academician and an eminent researcher - Rangaraj M. Rangayyan, PhD, PEng, FIEEE, FEIC, FAIMBE, FSPIE, FSIIM, FCMBES, FCAE, "University Professor", Professor, Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering, Schulich School of Engineering, University Drive N.W., Calgary, Alberta, Canada, for delivering key-note address. The chief guest Prof. D.K. Subramanian, Retd Professor of IISc, and also TEQIP Mentor of our college advised the research scholars to take up innovative experiments and perform meaningful research which will be useful for the society. Dr. Rangayyan highlighted the importance of research and gave a thought provoking lecture on Retinopathy. This was followed by an invited talk by Dr.K.N.Bhanuprakash, Scientist ASTAR, and Singapore on Electronic Brain Atlas. Dr.Rajkumar Vedam, Invensys, USA delivered a talk on Search Directions for Intelligence which exposed the various areas to take up the research. The sessions were held as per schedule. Nearly One hundred papers were presented by the research scholars. During the Valedictory function Sri. Sharan C Padashetty, Special Officer, TEQIP, DTE, Karnataka highlighted the financial assistance to various research activities. The papers received from various research scholars and students in and around the globe were of good quality. We had received around 260 papers out of which we had selected 118 papers. All the selected papers were presented and the deliberations were more useful to the research scholars. A number of researchers from both academic institutions and industries had contributed their novel methods, algorithms, analysis techniques to solve various problems in the above field. The innovative ideas of young researchers of various institutions were brought to lime light by sharing them with others in this platform.
